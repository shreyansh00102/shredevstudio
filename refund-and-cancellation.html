Refund & Cancellation

This policy explains the terms related to project cancellation, refunds, and service payments at Shre Dev Studio.

Section 01

Advance Payment

  • All projects require an advance payment before work begins.
  • The advance payment confirms the booking of your project and reserves development time.
🔄
Section 02

Refund Policy

  • Advance payments are generally non-refundable once work on the project has started.
  • If no work has been started and the cancellation request is approved, a partial refund may be considered after deducting any applicable administrative or transaction charges.
  • Payments made for domain registration, web hosting, SSL certificates, premium themes, plugins, APIs, software licenses, or other third-party services are non-refundable.
⚠️ Please note: Third-party purchases (domains, hosting, plugins, etc.) are always non-refundable as these are processed by external providers.
❌
Section 03

Project Cancellation

A client may request cancellation at any time. If cancellation occurs after development has started:

  • Charges will apply based on the work completed.
  • Any remaining unpaid amount for completed work may still be payable.
  • Project files, source code, designs, or deliverables will be handed over only after all outstanding payments are cleared.
⏳
Section 04

Delay by Client

If the client does not provide the required content, approvals, or feedback for an extended period, the project may be placed on hold. Extended delays may require a revised delivery schedule and additional charges.

💡 Tip: To keep your project on track, please provide content and feedback in a timely manner.
🚫
Section 05

Service Cancellation

Shre Dev Studio reserves the right to suspend or cancel services if:

  • False or misleading information is provided.
  • The service is requested for illegal, fraudulent, abusive, or prohibited activities.
  • Payment obligations are not fulfilled.
⚙️
Section 06

Refund Processing

Where a refund is approved, it will normally be processed using the original payment method within a reasonable processing period, subject to payment provider timelines.

🕐 Refund processing times may vary depending on your bank or payment provider. Typically, refunds are processed within 7–14 business days.
📧
Section 07

Contact

For refund or cancellation requests, please contact us using the details provided on the Contact Us page. We are committed to resolving your concerns promptly and fairly.
